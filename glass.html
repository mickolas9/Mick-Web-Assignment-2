UI Trends Year in Review

Using Glass to Create Memorable Design

Glass is a design trend that has been gaining popularity in recent years. It is a material that can be used to create a sense of depth and dimension in your designs.

Glass UI: The Clarity Trend

Glass UI, inspired by the look of frosted glass, utilizes background blur and transparency to create a sense of depth and layering, giving applications a lightweight, airy feel. This style leverages subtle reflections and light to add a touch of realism, fostering a clean and futuristic user environment that harmonizes with a wide range of design aesthetics.

Transparency in Interaction

The Glass UI trend continues to impress with its ability to overlay information while still providing a glimpse of what's underneath, fostering an environment where clarity and content coexist beautifully. This design trend plays with the idea of transparency, both literally and figuratively, encouraging open and light-filled digital spaces that seem to extend beyond the screen.
